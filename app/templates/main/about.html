About SmartHire

SmartHire is an automated resume screening and ranking system that uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and machine learning techniques to match job applicants with job descriptions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to streamline the hiring process by automating the initial screening of resumes, allowing recruiters to focus on the most qualified candidates and helping job seekers find positions that match their skills and experience.

How It Works

For Job Applicants

  1. Create an account and upload your resume (PDF or DOCX)
  2. Our system parses your resume and extracts key information
  3. Your resume is vectorized and stored for matching
  4. Recruiters can find your resume when it matches their job descriptions

For Recruiters

  1. Create an account and upload job descriptions
  2. Our system parses and vectorizes the job requirements
  3. Resumes are automatically ranked by relevance to your job
  4. View detailed insights about each candidate's match

Technology

SmartHire uses state-of-the-art NLP techniques including:

  • Resume parsing with spaCy and NLTK
  • Text vectorization using TF-IDF and word embeddings
  • Cosine similarity for matching resumes to job descriptions
  • Machine learning for continuous improvement of matching algorithms
